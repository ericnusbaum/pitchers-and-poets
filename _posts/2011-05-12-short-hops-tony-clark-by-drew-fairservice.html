Drew Fairservice, a Walkoff Walk veteran, blogs about the Blue Jays at the recently (and beautifully) redesigned Ghostrunner on First and other teams at The Score.

Tony Clark is the most 90s first basemen of all time.

He could switch hit. He owned a solid (albeit no-brainer) nickname. He hit home runs onto Trumbull Avenue. He was a bad fielder. He was athletic enough to play college basketball. He got hurt. He went away. Then he came back! Then he went on TV.
