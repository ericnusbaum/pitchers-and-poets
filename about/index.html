Eric Nusbaum and Ted Walker run Pitchers & Poets, a deep space vessel custom-built to explore the farthest corners of the baseball multiverse.

The name comes from Robert Frost, who said "Poets are like baseball pitchers. Both have their moments. The intervals are the tough things."


My name is Eric. I grew up in Los Angeles rooting for the Dodgers. Now I live in Seattle, where I write. Find me on Twitter @ericnus.  This is my other website.



I am Ted. I'm an Astros fan from Houston, Texas. I went to Middlebury College in Vermont, and I've got an MFA in Writing from SAIC. I'm on Twitter at@Ted_PandP. Learn more about me at tedwalker.net.

I'm Patrick.  I'm from the Pacific Northwest, and I'm almost finished getting my Masters in Education from the University of Washington.   I'm on Twitter at @euqubud.
